What is GiveWay?

GiveWay connects volunteer drivers with community members who need a meal. Our routing engine sorts requests by distance so every trip saves time, fuel, and food.

How to use:
1 · Click “Enter” to open the map.
2 · Students / recipients sign up to request food.
3 · Volunteers sign in, view nearby stops, and follow the optimized route.
